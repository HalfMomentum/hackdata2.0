Analysis of national pride projects of Nepal

Type of challenge
Open
Description of challenge

Many big projects (hydroelectricity, irrigation, bridge, highways etc) of Nepal consume huge amount of budget as a part of procurement. Although public are interested learning more about the progress, not everyone has an access to related data/information. Note that majority of the budget is spent through procurement of construction goods, works, services etc.


The challenge is to enable procurement data of such big projects that gives a picture of how a particular project is progressing facilitating stakeholders on how far a particular project is procuring goods, works and services that has an ultimate link with the accomplishment of project targets.

Look for
The existing electronic-procurement portals consists (not all) of certain information as:
  • Call for invitation
  • Type of works and specifications (Exact locations to some extent)
  • Bid Documents and Addendums (downloadable)
  • Last date of bid submission
  • Bid opening Date
  • Contract Start Date
  • Contract end date
  • Letters of Intent
  • Awardee (winning company) & Contract Amount
