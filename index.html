HackData 2.0?

Hackdata 2.0 is an inderdisciplinary and open-ended event that would bring together creative minds from a variety of fields under one roof. Engineers, business students, designers and enterpreneurs would together build solutions utilizing data science in useful and positive ways

A 30 hours event packed with sessions workshops and networking activities! Build a product using any dataset available in public domain and get a chance to take away home loads of swag and prizes!


Mission


The mission of HackData is to create a gender diverse and technologically inclined community. Our goal is to create opportunity for newcomers to explore a hackathon environment while learning and solving compelling problems.


There is a special award category for the all women's team that makes the best project! This should surely motivate females to form and work in all women's teams.


Flow of Events

1

Individuals with different skill sets come together to form a team (maximum of 3 individuals only) and take up one of the challenges

2

The teams will work on creating solutions such as visualization, web app or mobile app

3

The judge will visit each team, while they are working, and start pre-evaluating each team

4

Shortlisted teams will have 7 minutes to present their solutions

5

once again, judges will evaluate each team

6

The scores from the judges will be tallied


Am I eligible to participate

  • Data Analyst

  • Frontend Developer

  • Project Manager

  • Designer

  • Mobile Developer


If you know see yourself fitting in one of the above role than you are highly eligible to participate in hackdata 2.0

How to participate

  1. Fill the form attached in the website
  2. A few days later you will recieve an email. The email will instruct to confirm your participation or inform you that seats are full
  3. In your next email, you will recieve imoprtant instructions and information for the hackathon
  4. Once selected, make sure to choose one of the challenges, go through the data provided in the challenge and prepare your ammunitions (laptop, softwares, hardwares, plans etc) for the day
